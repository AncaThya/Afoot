Frames for your soul!

Hiking trails a step away from your door so get your camera ready!

Hi! My name is Anca Pirvu. I work full time and I am also a student. I was always excited about photography and cinematography, that's why I always tried to make time for them in the weekends or in vacation. I created this website so I can share with the world fantastic places to hike around Cluj-Napoca that brim with stunning sceneries and frames. I believe the most amazing pictures are the ones where you can capture the nature's beauty. I am so excited to share with you all the wonders of the nature through the lenses of my cameras. Don't be shy, photography is calling you. Enjoy the world and try to capture as much of it as possible. Keep it in your heart and always remember, beauty is all around us!

About

Afoot is a small website that provides accessible ideas for hikes around Cluj-Napoca during weekends and also shares beautiful photos from these locations. We encourage people to give a try to photography and also to try to discover nature as much as possible.If you find our content interesting and you would like to keep up with our adventures for future hikes ideas, please do not hesitate to follow us!
